About Velvetaire

Velvetaire Fragrances Inc., founded in 2023 in Los Angeles, is an avant-garde perfume house with a mission to redefine how we experience scent. Our creations go beyond traditional gender and luxury boundaries—embracing contradiction, curiosity, and the uncanny.

We collaborate with independent perfumers, contemporary artists, and scent theorists to engineer experiences that stir memory, discomfort, and wonder. Each product is both a fragrance and a philosophy in motion.

Velvetaire is where disruption meets delicacy—and fragrance becomes rebellion.

Meet Our CEO

Julien A. Verrier is a Paris-born olfactory radical and former cultural strategist. He holds a PhD in Olfactory Semiotics from the University of Florence and worked for global fashion houses before founding Velvetaire. Known for his controversial scent manifesto “Smell Before Sight,” Verrier believes that perfumes should challenge societal norms, not just enhance appearances.
